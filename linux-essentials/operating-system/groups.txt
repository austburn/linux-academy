Groups
------
- Let's say we have 20 users on our Linux system and groups of 5 need access to certain types of files - we can apply permissions at a group level and avoid adding file permissions five separate times
- /etc/group - group definitions
- /etc/gshadow - group passwords
- groupadd <options> <groupname>
    - similar to useradd: password, system groups, etc.
- groupmod
    - similar to usermod
    - groupmod -g 1010 mygroup
- groupdel
